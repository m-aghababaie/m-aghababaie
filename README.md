## What I Am Exploring

I am building a public portfolio of applied RL projects across:

- multi-armed bandits for inspection and recommendation decisions
- Markov Decision Processes for modelling buildings as decision environments
- dynamic programming for risk and energy optimisation
- Q-learning for maintenance, HVAC, and control problems
- policy-gradient methods for more advanced building intelligence
- RL-based decision systems for ESG, IoT, and loss prevention

## Why This Matters

Buildings are sequential decision environments.

Every sensor reading is a state.  
Every maintenance action is a decision.  
Every inspection, retrofit, alert, or HVAC adjustment creates a future consequence.

That is why Reinforcement Learning is a powerful framework for property and insurance intelligence.

I believe the next generation of insurance will not only price risk — it will actively reduce it.
