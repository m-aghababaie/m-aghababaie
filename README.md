## Why This Matters

Buildings are sequential decision environments.  
Every sensor reading is a state.  
Every maintenance action is a decision.  
Every inspection, retrofit, alert, or HVAC adjustment creates a future consequence.

Reinforcement Learning is the right framework for this problem.

**I believe the next generation of insurance will not only price risk —  
it will actively reduce it.**
